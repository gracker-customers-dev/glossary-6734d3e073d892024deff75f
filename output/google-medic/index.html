Google Medic

A major Google algorithm update that primarily affected health and wellness-related websites.

Description

The Google Medic update, rolled out in August 2018, was a significant algorithm change that impacted many websites in the health and wellness sector, though its effects were felt across various niches. This update emphasized the importance of E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) as critical ranking factors for content related to health, finance, and other YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) topics. Websites that lacked credible sources, expert opinions, or trustworthy information saw a decline in their rankings. Google aimed to improve the quality of search results by promoting content that demonstrated a higher level of expertise and reliability. This update highlighted the need for website owners to invest in creating high-quality content, ensuring it is backed by credible sources and written by knowledgeable authors. As a result, those in the digital marketing space were prompted to reassess their content strategies, particularly in sensitive areas where misinformation could lead to serious consequences for readers.

Examples

  • A health blog with poorly researched articles loses rankings.
  • An authoritative medical site with expert-written content sees improved visibility.

Additional Information

  • E-A-T became a focal point for SEO strategies post-update.
  • The update reinforced the importance of user trust in content creation.
