Authenticated Identifiers

Digital markers that verify the identity of users while ensuring their data privacy.

Description

Authenticated identifiers refer to unique identifiers that are linked to verified user accounts, enabling marketers to track and engage with users across various platforms while maintaining a level of privacy. These identifiers are often associated with user profiles that have been authenticated, meaning that the identity of the user has been confirmed through methods such as email verification, social media logins, or two-factor authentication. By using authenticated identifiers, businesses can create a more personalized and effective marketing strategy, as they can tailor their messages and offers to specific audience segments based on verified user behavior and preferences. Unlike traditional cookies, which can be easily deleted or blocked, authenticated identifiers provide a more reliable means for tracking user interactions. This is particularly important in an era where data privacy concerns are paramount, as authenticated identifiers help ensure compliance with regulations while fostering trust with users who are increasingly aware of their digital footprints.

Examples

  • Email addresses used for account creation on e-commerce sites.
  • Social media login credentials allowing access to personalized content.

Additional Information

  • Authenticated identifiers help build a more comprehensive customer profile.
  • They can enhance targeting efforts and improve return on investment (ROI) for marketing campaigns.
