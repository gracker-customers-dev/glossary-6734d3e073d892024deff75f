Google Grants

A program that provides free advertising credits to eligible nonprofit organizations to promote their missions on Google.

Description

Google Grants is part of the Google for Nonprofits program, which aims to support nonprofit organizations by providing them with tools and resources to enhance their online presence. Through Google Ads, eligible nonprofits can receive up to $10,000 per month in in-kind advertising to help promote their initiatives, attract donors, and raise awareness about their causes. The grants are intended to help organizations reach a broader audience and drive relevant traffic to their websites. To qualify for Google Grants, organizations must meet specific eligibility criteria, including being a registered nonprofit and adhering to Google's policies. Once accepted, nonprofits can create and manage text-based ads that appear on Google search results and gain insights into their campaign performance through Google Ads reporting tools. This program enables nonprofits to leverage digital marketing to fulfill their mission effectively and engage with potential supporters worldwide.

Examples

  • A local animal shelter uses Google Grants to promote adoption events and increase community engagement.
  • An environmental nonprofit utilizes Google Grants to raise awareness about climate change and drive traffic to their donation page.

Additional Information

  • Google Grants can only be used for text ads on the Google Search Network.
  • Organizations must regularly maintain their accounts and comply with Google’s policies to keep their grants active.
