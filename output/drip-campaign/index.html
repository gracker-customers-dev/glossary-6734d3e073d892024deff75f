Drip Campaign

A marketing strategy that involves sending a series of automated messages to prospects or customers over time.

Description

A drip campaign is a form of marketing automation where a series of pre-written messages are sent to contacts over a specified period. These messages can take various forms, including emails, SMS, or other forms of communication. The main goal of a drip campaign is to nurture leads and guide them through the sales funnel by providing relevant information at timed intervals. Drip campaigns can be tailored based on user behavior, such as actions taken on a website, previous purchases, or demographic information. This allows marketers to deliver highly targeted content that resonates with the recipient's interests and needs. Drip campaigns can help increase engagement, build relationships, and ultimately drive conversions by keeping the brand top-of-mind for the audience. They are often used for onboarding new customers, re-engaging inactive users, or promoting new products and services.

Examples

  • A welcome email series for new subscribers to introduce them to a brand's offerings.
  • Follow-up emails to leads who downloaded a free resource, guiding them toward a purchase.

Additional Information

  • Drip campaigns are commonly used in email marketing but can also be applied to SMS and social media.
  • They can be automated using marketing automation platforms, making it easier to manage large-scale campaigns.
